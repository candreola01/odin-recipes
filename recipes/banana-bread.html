Banana Bread

This banana bread is the perfect amount of moist, taste super good, and is also healthy!

Instructions:

  1. Generously greese a pan with olive oil
  2. Mix the olive oil, brown sugar, eggs, and mashed bananas together
  3. Add baking soda, salt, vanilla, and cinnamon
  4. Mix whole wheat and white flour
  5. Pour into the pan and bake!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar packed
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 bananas (mashed, about 2 cups)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
