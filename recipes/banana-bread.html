Banana Bread

This banana bread is the perfect amount of moist, taste super good, and is also healthy!

Using olive oil helps keep it moist, as does using LOTS of bananas.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar packed
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 bananas (mashed, about 2 cups)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Use about 2 tablespoons of olive oil to generously grease the bottom and the sides of a laof pan. it's OK if some pools at the bottom.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the remaining olive oil (just under 1/2 cup) with the brown sugar (1/2 cup)
  4. Add the eggs and mix for about 1 minute
  5. Add the bananas and mix together
  6. Stir in the baking soda, kosher salt, cinnamon, and vanilla extract (1 teaspoon of each)
  7. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
  8. Allow to cool in pan for at least 10 minutes.
