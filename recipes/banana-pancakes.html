Banana Pancakes

Wake up on the right side of the bed wiith a stack of sweet, cozy, and simple banana pancakes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
